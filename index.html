Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have both Retail and Wholesale customers?
Yes. The system is "Hybrid". Regular customers see standard retail prices. When a Wholesale partner logs in, the system automatically detects their tag and reveals the discounted wholesale prices and bulk purchase rules.

How is VAT Exemption handled?
The system includes a VIES validation checker. When a European business enters their VAT ID at checkout/registration, the system checks if it's valid. If confirmed, the 21% tax is automatically removed from their total.

What payment gateways are included?
We configure Shopify Payments (Credit Cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay) and PayPal. For B2B clients, we can also enable "Net Terms" (pay later via Bank Transfer) or Cash on Delivery (COD) specifically for Spain.

Do I need a separate website for B2B?
No. This proposal consolidates everything into one single website. This saves you money on maintenance and makes inventory management much easier since stock is shared between retail and wholesale.
